January 2024

The Cold War and Its Battle for Global Ideological Dominance

This article explores the Cold War, a pivotal period of the 20th century characterized by a deep ideological conflict between capitalism, supported by the US, and socialism, supported by the USSR. It looks at the rivalry's effects on the world at large, including espionage, the nuclear weapons race, proxy conflicts, and the forming of military alliances. The essay also looks at how the Cold War influenced global affairs, left a geopolitical legacy, and still has an impact on the contemporary world.
